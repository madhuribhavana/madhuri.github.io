CURRENT METHODS

The paper usually provides location of items using aisle and shelf numbers, which is not intuitive and requires the picker to refer back and forth to the paper. Paper-based systems are still the most widely used method for order picking in warehouses today.

NEED OF THE HOUR

Due to the impact that operational costs have on businesses, a more time efficient and accurate order picking process is the need of the hour. Such an intervention shall also decrease the fatigue of workers and hence prevent man-made errors.

PAST RESEARCH SUGGESTS...

HMDs can increase the speed of order picking by 37% compared to a paper based method. Hence, we proposed a Augmented Reality (AR) based solution.
